Sign up for Maik3r SMS

Maik3r · SMS-first operations tools for general contractors

Maik3r helps small general contractors and their field workers log receipts, record labor hours, and exchange invoice notifications by text message. If your contractor uses Maik3r, this page explains how to enroll your phone number to receive job-related SMS messages.

To enroll, text JOIN to +1 (424) 577-3220 from your mobile phone.

You will receive a confirmation message describing the SMS service and asking you to reply YES to complete enrollment. Once enrolled, reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe.

What you'll receive

Once enrolled, the Maik3r number will send you:

  • Receipt confirmations — when you text a photo of a job-site receipt, you'll get a short reply confirming the vendor, amount, and project it was logged to.
  • Labor reminders — a morning text asking which job you're on, and an end-of-day text asking what time you wrapped.
  • Invoice notifications — when an invoice is generated for a project you worked on.

How to enroll (step by step)

  1. Open the messages app on your mobile phone.
  2. Send the word JOIN to +1 (424) 577-3220.
  3. You'll receive a confirmation message describing the service. It will note that message frequency varies, that standard message and data rates may apply, and that you can reply STOP at any time.
  4. Reply YES to confirm enrollment. Your consent is recorded with a timestamp at this moment.
  5. You'll receive a short welcome message. You're now enrolled.

How to opt out

Reply STOP (or any of UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, STOPALL, REVOKE) to the Maik3r number at any time. You will stop receiving messages immediately. To re-enroll later, text JOIN again.

Help

Reply HELP to the Maik3r number for support, or email Spokes@maik3r.com.

Message frequency, rates, and privacy

Message frequency varies based on your job activity. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. Maik3r does not sell or share your mobile number for marketing purposes.
